introduction

get to know a little about the people in charge

We are Oxford Academy STEM, an on campus club and academic team. We aim to support the bright young minds of the students within our school. Our goal for students is simple: To learn, compete, and thrive. Members of OA STEM participate in STEM related competitions, which range from test taking building robots which compete against others. Don't worry if you don't feel ready for competition, we welcome in students without experience because education is our number one priority. You can be a part of one, or all four of our departments!

when + where

know were to go and when to come

Each department will meet once a week, with the exception of Engineering, which meets twice a week, over the six week period of June 13nd and July 30st. This allows students to choose which departments they are interested in attending.

Oxford Academy, 5172 Orange Ave Cypress, CA | Room 306
